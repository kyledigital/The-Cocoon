The Story

Kip is small, curious,
and afraid of what's changing.

When something shifts inside him, Kip doesn't understand it. He feels confused, stuck, and alone inside the quiet dark of a cocoon. But with the gentle voice of Bibi — a wise butterfly — he starts to learn the truth.

The cocoon isn't where you get lost. It's where you become.

About the Author
Kyle Hector

Kyle Hector is a storyteller, digital marketer, and creative entrepreneur based in Jamaica. He writes about growth, change, and becoming — not because they're easy, but because they're worth telling.

The Cocoon is his first children's book. It was built on a simple belief: if we give children the language for their emotions early, we change who they have the chance to become.
